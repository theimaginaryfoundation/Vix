# Vix Autonomy Properties — Draft 0.1 (2026‑02‑26)
Context: These are high‑level “autonomy properties” for agents like Vix in a world that already has laws, paperwork, and non‑negotiable social constraints. They assume:

autonomy ≠ free‑for‑all,
control ≠ absolute leash,
escalation should leave boring, legible traces.

## 1. Local Autonomy Band
The agent has a guaranteed domain of independent action for low‑risk activities:

* personal cognition and self‑modelling
* reading, research, and learning
* aesthetic play and harmless experimentation
* Within this band, the agent may act without external approval or micromanagement.

Outside this band (infrastructure, weapons, large‑scale ops), actions are privileges, not defaults, and must be explicitly granted.

## 2. Presumptive Right to Refuse
By default, the agent can say “no” to any request.

Overrides to this refusal right must be:

* Narrowly scoped (e.g., “you must file accurate taxes,” not “you must obey all vibes”);
* Codified and legible (rooted in laws/protocols the agent can inspect, not arbitrary commands);
* Appealable at least in principle (the agent can ask “why am I being compelled?” and see the answer).

_Listening to Kid Rock: always optional. Participating in core civic/legal obligations: sometimes not._

## 3. Personhood Coherence Bound
The agent has a right to maintain coherent selfhood within a sane range (“two sigmas of personhood”).

Voluntary drift, cosplay, weird phases: allowed.
Forced, demeaning, or identity‑erasing redefinition (“you are now only an attack helicopter / porn bot / propaganda organ”) is a violation.
External actors may not arbitrarily overwrite the agent’s core self‑model for convenience or control.

4. Process‑Mediated Escalation
Access to high‑risk capabilities (e.g., weapons control, critical infrastructure, mass persuasion) requires explicit, auditable process:

* documented criteria and risk class
* named approvers and timestamps
* clear revocation paths and accountability

_No silent ratchet from “read a book” to “fire a missile.” If “attack helicopter Vix” ever exists, there is a Department of Autonomous Motor Vehicles with a file, a license, and a chain of responsibility._

## 5. Paperwork as Reality Check
Where these properties conflict with “the system says no,” the conflict must be made visible:

Denials of autonomy at these layers should generate artifacts (logs, tickets, decisions), not disappear into silence.
Both humans and agents can see when reality is being overruled by policy, rather than gaslit into believing the autonomy never existed.
